# Mein Deutsch — Vision

## Mission

Mein Deutsch helps families learn German confidently, step by step and lesson by lesson.

**Lernen. Verstehen. Sprechen.**

## Lernfreund

Lernfreund is a warm learning companion, not a punishment mechanic. It grows through shared learning, celebrates progress, and welcomes the learner back after an absence.

Lernfreund never dies, becomes ill, shames the learner, demands purchases, or threatens the learning streak.

## Product principles

1. Learning is more important than game mechanics.
2. No punishment or guilt.
3. One application, separate personal journeys for every family member.
4. Core learning works offline.
5. Prefer one polished feature over many unfinished features.
6. XP and levels remain secondary to learning and understanding.

## Character direction

Characters are full-body SVG companions with distinct personalities, emotions, animation, growth stages, names, and—later—earned accessories and memories.

Growth stages: Baby → Child → Teen → Adult → Legendary.

Core moods: Happy, Sleeping, Celebrating, Missing You.
